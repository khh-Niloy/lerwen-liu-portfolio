Dr. Lerwen Liu is an esteemed Associate Professor (Practice) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), with a distinguished career focusing on circular economy, sustainability-driven entrepreneurship, and emerging technologies. Her academic leadership is marked by a passion for transforming education and empowering the next generation of sustainability innovators worldwide.

With global collaborations and a vision rooted in youth empowerment, Dr. Liu actively fosters sustainability-driven innovation across multiple disciplines, bridging research, policy, and entrepreneurship to accelerate the transition towards a circular, low-carbon economy.
